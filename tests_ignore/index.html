About Us

Bella's Pizzeria has been serving the community since 1995. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, from locally sourced vegetables to hand-stretched dough made daily in our kitchen.

Whether you’re dining in, taking out, or ordering delivery, we promise a pizza experience that feels just like Italy — warm, friendly, and full of flavor!
